Human motion recognition task

Posted on 2022-03-25 In deep learning

Action recognition task overview

The methods used in motion recognition tasks are generally divided into two kinds. One is based on the traditional manual feature extraction method, which is mainly based on the detection and representation of interest points, and uses gradient histogram, spatio-temporal interest points, improved dense trajectories and other features to classify. However, this method lacks certain flexibility and scalability; The other method is the method of automatic feature extraction based on deep learning, which is also the mainstream method in the field of motion recognition at this stage. It mainly has three branches: dual stream convolution neural network, LSTM and 3D convolution neural network. Different network models have different characteristics and advantages.

Bone based behavior recognition model ST-GCN and AGCN

Posted on 2022-03-25 In deep learning

ST-GCN

Dynamic bone data represented by two-dimensional / three-dimensional coordinates of human joints has been widely studied in human motion recognition because of its high-level semantic information and environmental robustness. ST-GCN is a classic bone based behavior recognition model proposed by AAAI 2018. By applying graph convolution to human bone data with topological structure and using spatio-temporal graph convolution to extract spatio-temporal features for behavior recognition, the accuracy of bone based behavior recognition task is greatly improved.

java锁

Posted on 2021-11-02 Edited on 2022-02-20 In java

在学习或者使用Java的过程中进程会遇到各种各样的锁的概念：公平锁、非公平锁、自旋锁、可重入锁、偏向锁、轻量级锁、重量级锁、读写锁、互斥锁等。这里整理了Java中的各种锁。

公平锁和非公平锁

公平锁是指多个线程在等待同一个锁时，必须按照申请锁的先后顺序来一次获得锁。

公平锁的好处是等待锁的线程不会饿死，但是整体效率相对低一些；非公平锁的好处是整体效率相对高一些，但是有些线程可能会饿死或者说很早就在等待锁，但要等很久才会获得锁。其中的原因是公平锁是严格按照请求所的顺序来排队获得锁的，而非公平锁时可以抢占的，即如果在某个时刻有线程需要获取锁，而这个时候刚好锁可用，那么这个线程会直接抢占，而这时阻塞在等待队列的线程则不会被唤醒。
